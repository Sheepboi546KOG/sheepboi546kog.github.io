Privacy Policy — KOG (Kleiner Oil Group)

Last Updated: 08/03/2026 (DD/MM/YY, GMT)

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we (KOG) collect, use, store, and manage information related to KOG Bot. By using our services, interacting with the bot, or attending KOG events, you accept the practices described below.

2. Scope

This policy applies to anyone who:

  • Uses or interacts with the bot;
  • Participates in communities, servers, or events managed by KOG;
  • Is subject to moderation, compliance, or background checks by authorized staff.

3. What we collect

3.1 Data created by user interactions

We collect data only when you interact with the bot or participate in an event. Stored data includes:

  • userId — Discord identifier (unique)
  • eventsAttended — number of events attended
  • eventsHosted — number of events hosted
  • merits — points or rewards
  • trainingsAttended — number of trainings
  • squadAttended, squadMerit, squadHosted — squad-related counts (only when squad logging command is used)
  • robloxId / username — Roblox identifier for Roblox-related commands
  • Discord Roles — for quota management or other miscellaneous commands

We do not collect IP addresses, device identifiers, payment information, or private DMs unless explicitly submitted to the bot.

3.2 Moderation and disciplinary records

We maintain moderation records tied to user IDs. Records may include:

  • Warnings, bans, strike reasons
  • Moderator notes
  • Timestamps
  • Evidence such as message excerpts or screenshots

These records are stored for moderation, accountability, and safety.

4. Event attendance and logging

When you attend or register for a KOG event, the bot may log attendance and update the fields in §3.1. Logging occurs only when you interact with the bot or an event command triggers recording.

5. Moderation, verification, and compliance checks

Authorized staff and automated tools may perform checks using data from our managed environments or publicly available platform data. Checks may be performed by:

  • Designated moderators
  • Authorized staff
  • HR personnel, acting under internal policies and discretion
  • Automated moderation systems

HR may run checks when reasonably necessary for personnel management, dispute resolution, or rule enforcement. Third-party/public checks may include publicly accessible platform data and trusted third-party tools as part of investigations.

6. Types of checks we may perform

  • Review messages sent within KOG-managed channels for rule enforcement
  • Query a user’s public Roblox data for blacklisted or policy-restricted items
  • Inspect public friends lists for associations with blacklisted accounts
  • Review public group memberships for restricted groups
  • Query public API endpoints for profiles, friends, inventory, or group data
  • Consult third-party moderation databases, e.g., Rotector

All checks are limited to public data or actions within KOG-managed environments. No illicit or private access methods are used.

7. Moderation logs, archival, and deletion policy

  • Moderation and administrative logs (including evidence) are retained for accountability, security, and archival purposes
  • Moderation logs may not be deleted if required for investigations, legal obligations, or safety
  • Non-essential data (e.g., event counts or merits) may be removed on verified request, unless retention is required
  • Deletion requests may result in loss of event access, leaderboard placements, or moderation history

8. Historical data and retroactive disclosure

This policy documents practices that may have been in use before publication. You may request review or removal of historical data subject to §7. KOG is not liable for omissions, inaccuracies, or consequences from missed, incomplete, or incorrectly recorded data.

10. Data sharing

We do not sell, trade, or commercially distribute personal information. Data may be shared only when necessary for:

  • Legal compliance
  • Protection of the service, users, or safety
  • Operation through trusted infrastructure providers
  • Moderation/background check info may be shared with QSOC, QIAD, or other communities under KOG HR/KIAD HR discretion

11. Data retention

Records are retained only as long as reasonably necessary for operational, moderation, security, and archival purposes. Retention periods vary depending on record type and operational requirements.
