Privacy Policy — KOG (Kleiner Oil Group)

Last Updated: 08/03/2026 (DD/MM/YY, GMT)

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how KOG (Kleiner Oil Group) collects, uses, stores, and manages information related to the KOG Bot and KOG-managed environments.

By interacting with the bot, participating in KOG events, or using services within KOG-managed communities, you acknowledge and accept the practices described in this policy.

2. Scope

This policy applies to individuals who:

  • Interact with the KOG Bot
  • Participate in communities, servers, or events managed by KOG
  • Are subject to moderation, compliance, or background checks performed by authorized staff

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process data under the following lawful bases where applicable:

  • Legitimate Interest — maintaining community safety, moderation, event management, and bot functionality
  • User Consent — when users voluntarily submit information through commands or verification systems
  • Platform Compliance — ensuring compliance with platform rules and community policies

4. Information We Collect

4.1 Data Created Through Bot Interactions

We collect data when users interact with the bot or participate in events. Stored information may include:

  • userId — Discord identifier (unique)
  • eventsAttended — number of events attended
  • eventsHosted — number of events hosted
  • merits — reward or point values
  • trainingsAttended — number of trainings attended
  • squadAttended, squadMerit, squadHosted — squad-related participation metrics
  • robloxId / username — Roblox identifier used for Roblox-related commands
  • Discord Roles — used for quota management, permissions, or bot functionality

We do not collect IP addresses, device identifiers, payment information, or private direct messages unless explicitly submitted to the bot by a user.

4.2 Moderation and Disciplinary Records

We maintain moderation records tied to Discord user IDs. These records may include:

  • Warnings, bans, and strike reasons
  • Moderator notes
  • Timestamps of moderation actions
  • Evidence such as message excerpts or screenshots

These records are maintained for moderation accountability, safety, and community management.

5. Event Attendance and Logging

When you attend or register for a KOG event, the bot may log attendance and update fields listed in Section 4.1.

Logging occurs only when:

  • A user interacts with the bot
  • An event command triggers recording
  • Authorized staff use logging commands

6. Moderation, Verification, and Compliance Checks

Authorized staff and automated systems may perform checks using information from KOG-managed environments or publicly available platform data.

Checks may be performed by:

  • Designated moderators
  • Authorized staff members
  • Human Resources HR personnel acting under internal policies

These checks may occur when reasonably necessary for moderation, dispute resolution, personnel management, background checks, or rule enforcement.

7. Types of Checks We May Perform

  • Review message counts within KOG-managed channels for rule enforcement
  • Query public Roblox profile data for restricted or blacklisted items
  • Inspect public friends lists for associations with blacklisted accounts
  • Review public group memberships for restricted groups
  • Query public API endpoints related to profiles, friends, inventory, or group membership
  • Consult third-party moderation databases such as Rotector

All checks are limited to publicly available data or activity within KOG-managed environments. No private or unauthorized access methods are used.

8. Moderation Logs, Archival, and Deletion Policy

  • Moderation and administrative records may be retained for accountability, security, and archival purposes.
  • Moderation logs may not be deleted if required for investigations, rule enforcement, legal obligations, or community safety, unless proven to be inaccurate.
  • Non-essential operational data (such as event counts or merit totals) may be removed upon verified request unless retention is required.

Deleting certain data may affect:

  • Event participation history
  • Leaderboard placements
  • Access to certain services or roles

9. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect stored data. Access to stored information is restricted to authorized personnel and maintained within secure hosting environments.

While reasonable measures are taken to protect data, no system can guarantee absolute security.

10. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or commercially distribute personal information.

Data may be shared only when reasonably necessary for:

  • Legal compliance
  • Protection of the service, users, or community safety
  • Operation of the service through trusted infrastructure providers
  • Moderation cooperation between communities

Moderation, event data and background check information may be shared with QSOC, QIAD, or other affiliated communities at the discretion of KOG HR or KIAD HR when relevant to moderation or safety concerns.

11. Data Retention

Records are retained only as long as reasonably necessary for operational, moderation, security, or archival purposes. Retention periods may vary depending on the type of record and operational needs.

12. User Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, users may have the right to:

  • Request access to stored data
  • Request correction of inaccurate records
  • Request deletion of non-essential data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing activities where applicable

Requests may be subject to moderation, safety, or legal retention requirements.

13. Age Requirement

KOG services and the KOG Bot are intended for users 13 years of age or older in accordance with Discord’s Terms of Service.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. When changes occur, the Last Updated date will be modified. Continued use of the bot or services indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
