6. Types of Moderation Checks

Moderation or compliance reviews may include the following processes where relevant to enforcement or verification.

6.1 Platform Message Review

Authorized staff may review messages sent by users within Discord servers or environments operated or administered by [Organization / Project Name] for purposes including:

  • Enforcement of community rules
  • Investigation of reports or suspected violations
  • Maintenance of safety and moderation standards

This review is limited to communications occurring within environments under our administration.

6.2 Public Roblox Inventory Review

Using publicly available information from the Roblox platform, staff or automated systems may review a user’s public Roblox inventory to identify:

  • Clothing or assets designated as restricted or blacklisted under community policies
  • Items associated with prohibited or policy-restricted content

6.3 Roblox Friends List Review

Moderation systems or authorized personnel may review a user’s public Roblox friends list to determine whether the account maintains associations with individuals designated as blacklisted or restricted accounts under community policies.

6.4 Roblox Group Membership Review

Moderation checks may review public Roblox group memberships associated with a user’s account in order to identify membership in groups classified as restricted or blacklisted under internal moderation policies.

6.5 Public API-Based Data Verification
